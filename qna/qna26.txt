What is the primary role of app.js in the client-side architecture?
It acts as the connection to the UI, handling interactions like calling methods such as app.search from HTML elements

What is the main responsibility of API.js?
It centralizes making requests to the backend, parsing responses, and handling HTTP status codes like 200 or errors

In the server-side architecture, what are the key responsibilities of main.go?
It serves as the entry point of the application, sets up the server, connects to the database, reads connection strings, and sets up handlers

What is the role of a handler in the server-side architecture?
Handlers manage HTTP requests, read incoming requests, determine the appropriate response based on routes, and communicate with model repositories to retrieve or process data

What is the purpose of the repository in the data package?
Repositories make direct queries to the database and return data back to handlers in the form of models or slices of models
