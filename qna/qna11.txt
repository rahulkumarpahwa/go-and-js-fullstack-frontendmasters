What are the primary tables in the proposed database structure?
Users, movies, genre, actors, user_movies, movie_cast, movie_genre, and movie keywords tables

What is the relationship between users and movies in the database schema?
It is a one-to-many (1:n) relationship, where one user can have multiple movies

What are some potential benefits of using movie keywords in the database?
Keywords can help find similar movies by matching common themes or characteristics, such as identifying movies with similar historical periods or genres

What are some recommended PostgreSQL database providers mentioned?
Supabase, aiven.io, and neon.tech are recommended providers with free plan options

What does the install.go script do in the database setup process?
It connects to the database, removes comments from the SQL file, splits statements by semicolon, and executes each statement to create tables and populate data
