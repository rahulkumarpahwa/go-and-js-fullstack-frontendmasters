What are the two types of collections that users can have in the database?
Favorites and watchlist


What is the primary security measure for storing passwords in this application?
Hashing passwords instead of storing them in plain text

How can a system verify a user's password if the original password cannot be retrieved?
By hashing the entered password and comparing it to the stored hash

What is the login identifier that will be used in this application?
Email

What are the key database fields for a user account?
ID, name, email, hashed password, last login, time created, time confirmed, time deleted
