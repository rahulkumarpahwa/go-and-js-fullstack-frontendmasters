What does the asterisk on an integer parameter signify in database queries?
It indicates that the parameter is nullable or optional, meaning the value can be absent or not provided

What is the difference between 'like' and 'ilike' in Postgres?
ILike is similar to like, but performs case-insensitive matching of strings

What does fetchMovieRelation method do in the MovieRepository?
It retrieves additional movie-related information such as actors and keywords, and attaches this data to the movie model

Why might a developer choose to split database queries instead of using a single large query?
To maintain clean, readable code, even if a single large query might be faster to execute

What additional data is retrieved when getting movie details beyond basic movie metadata?
Movie details can include actors, full cast, and keywords, which require additional queries to other related database tables
